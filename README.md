<h1>Smart Waiter</h1>
<p>This is a meal storage and management app for everyone.</p>

<p>The main functions of this application</p>
- Stores the dish and its characteristics<br>
- Calculate the total amount to pay, total time, total calories of your meal.<br>
- Offer options according to your needs. <br>
- Make a list of three-course meals (soup, salad, main course) according to how much money you have or how many calories you need or the total time you spend cooking them.<br>
